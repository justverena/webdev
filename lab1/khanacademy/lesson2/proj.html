Hawaii!

Hawaii is an island state in the Western United States, about 3,200 km from the U.S. mainland in the Pacific Ocean. It is the only U.S. state outside North America , the only one which is an archipelago, and the only one in the tropics.

List of major Hawaii islands:

  • Hawaiʻi
  • Maui
  • Kahoʻolawe
  • Lānaʻi
  • Molokaʻi
  • Oʻahu
  • Kauaʻi
  • Niʻihau
